## Objective
Compare performance between two business regions using hypothesis testing.

## Method
- Independent t-test to compare mean Sales between two regions (North vs South).
- Null Hypothesis (H0): No difference in mean sales.
- Alternate Hypothesis (H1): Mean sales are different.
